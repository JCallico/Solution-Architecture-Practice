# Service Offerings

## Overview

The Solution Architecture Practice provides a comprehensive suite of services to support technology initiatives across the organization. Our offerings are designed to enable success at every stage of the solution lifecycle.

## Service Catalog

### 1. Strategic Architecture Services

#### Architecture Strategy & Roadmapping
- **Description**: Long-term technology strategy and multi-year architecture roadmaps
- **When to Use**: Annual planning, major technology transformations, platform evolution
- **Deliverables**: Architecture vision, roadmap, target state diagrams, migration plans
- **Timeline**: 4-12 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Dedicated architect assignment

#### Technology Assessment & Selection
- **Description**: Evaluation of technologies, platforms, and vendor solutions
- **When to Use**: Major technology decisions, platform selection, build vs. buy decisions
- **Deliverables**: Assessment report, recommendations, proof of concepts, decision records
- **Timeline**: 2-8 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Architecture review with specialists

#### Enterprise Integration Strategy
- **Description**: Integration architecture and API strategy across the enterprise
- **When to Use**: Multiple systems integration, API platform planning, data integration
- **Deliverables**: Integration architecture, API strategy, reference architecture
- **Timeline**: 6-16 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Integration architect assignment

### 2. Solution Design Services

#### Solution Architecture Design
- **Description**: End-to-end architecture design for new solutions and major enhancements
- **When to Use**: New product development, major feature releases, system replacements
- **Deliverables**: Solution architecture document, architecture diagrams, ADRs, NFR specifications
- **Timeline**: 2-12 weeks depending on scope
- **Engagement Model**: Embedded architect

#### Architecture Modernization
- **Description**: Legacy system modernization and cloud migration architecture
- **When to Use**: Legacy modernization, cloud migration, re-architecture initiatives
- **Deliverables**: Current state analysis, target architecture, migration strategy, roadmap
- **Timeline**: 4-16 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Modernization specialist

#### Data Architecture Design
- **Description**: Data modeling, data lake/warehouse architecture, analytics platforms
- **When to Use**: Data platform initiatives, analytics solutions, data governance
- **Deliverables**: Data architecture, data models, data flow diagrams, governance model
- **Timeline**: 4-12 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Data architect

#### Security Architecture Design
- **Description**: Security architecture, threat modeling, compliance design
- **When to Use**: Security-critical systems, compliance requirements, zero-trust implementation
- **Deliverables**: Security architecture, threat models, security controls, compliance mapping
- **Timeline**: 2-8 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Security architect collaboration

### 3. Architecture Review & Governance

#### Architecture Review Board (ARB)
- **Description**: Formal architecture reviews for major initiatives
- **When to Use**: All major projects, significant architecture changes, new platforms
- **Deliverables**: Review feedback, approval/conditional approval, action items
- **Timeline**: 1-2 week review cycle
- **Engagement Model**: ARB submission process

#### Design Review Sessions
- **Description**: Collaborative design review and feedback sessions
- **When to Use**: During solution design, before implementation, at key milestones
- **Deliverables**: Review notes, feedback, recommendations
- **Timeline**: 1-2 hours per session
- **Engagement Model**: Scheduled review sessions

#### Architecture Health Checks
- **Description**: Assessment of existing system architecture against best practices
- **When to Use**: Quarterly reviews, pre-scaling, major version updates
- **Deliverables**: Health check report, recommendations, remediation plan
- **Timeline**: 1-3 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Architect-led assessment

### 4. Advisory & Consulting

#### Architecture Advisory
- **Description**: On-demand architecture guidance and consultation
- **When to Use**: Quick questions, design discussions, pattern guidance
- **Deliverables**: Recommendations, references, example implementations
- **Timeline**: Ad-hoc, typically 30 min - 2 hours
- **Engagement Model**: Office hours, Slack support, scheduled calls

#### Proof of Concept (POC) Support
- **Description**: Architecture guidance for proof of concepts and prototypes
- **When to Use**: Evaluating new technologies, validating approaches
- **Deliverables**: POC architecture, success criteria, evaluation framework
- **Timeline**: 1-4 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Part-time architect support

#### Architecture Workshops
- **Description**: Facilitated workshops for design thinking and architecture planning
- **When to Use**: Kickoff events, design sprints, problem-solving sessions
- **Deliverables**: Workshop outcomes, diagrams, decisions, action items
- **Timeline**: Half-day to multi-day workshops
- **Engagement Model**: Facilitated sessions

### 5. Enablement & Knowledge Transfer

#### Architecture Training
- **Description**: Custom training on architecture patterns, practices, and tools
- **When to Use**: Team upskilling, onboarding, new pattern adoption
- **Deliverables**: Training materials, hands-on labs, certifications
- **Timeline**: 2 hours to multi-day programs
- **Engagement Model**: Scheduled training sessions

#### Documentation & Runbooks
- **Description**: Architecture documentation, runbooks, and playbooks
- **When to Use**: Post-implementation, knowledge transfer, operational handoff
- **Deliverables**: Architecture documentation, diagrams, runbooks, ADRs
- **Timeline**: 1-4 weeks
- **Engagement Model**: Documentation specialist

#### Community of Practice
- **Description**: Architecture community events, forums, and knowledge sharing
- **When to Use**: Ongoing learning, networking, sharing innovations
- **Deliverables**: Monthly meetups, tech talks, architecture blog posts
- **Timeline**: Ongoing monthly cadence
- **Engagement Model**: Community participation

## How to Engage

### 1. Determine Your Need
Review the service catalog to identify which service best fits your requirements.

### 2. Submit a Request
Use the [Architecture Services Request Form](../processes/service-request-form.md) or reach out via:
- Email: solution-architecture@company.com
- Slack: #architecture-requests
- ServiceNow: Architecture Services catalog

### 3. Initial Consultation
An architect will schedule a consultation to:
- Understand your requirements
- Recommend appropriate services
- Estimate timeline and effort
- Assign resources

### 4. Engagement
Depending on the service:
- Dedicated architect assignment for major projects
- Scheduled review sessions for ARB
- Office hours for advisory services
- Workshops and training as scheduled

### 5. Delivery & Follow-up
- Deliverables provided as defined
- Review and feedback sessions
- Knowledge transfer
- Post-engagement support

## Prioritization Criteria

When demand exceeds capacity, we prioritize based on:
1. **Strategic Alignment**: Alignment with company strategic objectives
2. **Business Impact**: Revenue impact, customer impact, risk mitigation
3. **Regulatory/Compliance**: Regulatory deadlines, compliance requirements
4. **Dependencies**: Blocking other teams or initiatives
5. **Technical Risk**: Complexity, novelty, scale, security concerns
