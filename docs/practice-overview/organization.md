# Organization & Leadership

## Practice Leadership

### Director of Solution Architecture
**Role**: Practice leader responsible for overall strategy, capability development, and delivery excellence.

**Responsibilities**:
- Define practice strategy and vision
- Build and develop the architecture team
- Establish architecture standards and governance
- Partner with executive leadership on technology strategy
- Manage architecture practice budget and resources
- Drive architecture community and culture

### Principal Architects
**Role**: Senior technical leaders providing strategic architecture guidance and mentorship.

**Responsibilities**:
- Lead major strategic initiatives
- Define architecture patterns and standards
- Mentor solution architects
- Represent architecture in executive forums
- Drive innovation and technical strategy
- Conduct architecture reviews

## Operating Model

### Hub and Spoke Model
We operate using a hub and spoke model:

**Hub (Center of Excellence)**:
- Standards and governance
- Patterns and reusable components
- Training and enablement
- Strategic architecture
- Architecture review board

**Spokes (Embedded Architects)**:
- Dedicated to product/platform teams
- Day-to-day architecture guidance
- Solution design and delivery
- Team collaboration
- Feedback to hub

## Roles & Responsibilities

### Solution Architect
**Level**: Individual Contributor / Technical Lead

**Responsibilities**:
- Design end-to-end solutions
- Create architecture documentation
- Collaborate with engineering teams
- Participate in architecture reviews
- Mentor junior architects
- Stay current with technology trends

**Required Skills**:
- 5+ years software development experience
- Strong system design capabilities
- Cloud platform expertise
- Excellent communication skills
- Domain expertise in assigned area

### Senior Solution Architect
**Level**: Senior Individual Contributor

**Responsibilities**:
- Lead complex solution designs
- Define architecture patterns
- Conduct architecture reviews
- Mentor solution architects
- Drive cross-team initiatives
- Represent architecture to stakeholders

**Required Skills**:
- 8+ years experience including 3+ as architect
- Deep expertise in multiple domains
- Proven track record of complex solutions
- Strong leadership and influence
- Strategic thinking

### Principal Architect
**Level**: Senior Technical Leader

**Responsibilities**:
- Set technical direction for domain
- Define standards and patterns
- Lead strategic initiatives
- Mentor architecture team
- Partner with executive leadership
- Drive innovation

**Required Skills**:
- 12+ years experience including 5+ as architect
- Recognized expert in domain
- Strategic technology vision
- Executive communication skills
- Thought leadership

## Governance Structure

### Architecture Review Board (ARB)
**Composition**:
- Director of Solution Architecture (Chair)
- Principal Architects (Core Members)
- Domain Experts (As Needed)
- Security & Compliance Representatives

**Meeting Cadence**: Weekly

**Scope**: Review and approve high-impact architecture decisions

### Architecture Council
**Composition**:
- All architects in the practice
- Special guests and speakers

**Meeting Cadence**: Monthly

**Scope**: Knowledge sharing, standards review, community building

### Architecture Office Hours
**Host**: Rotating architects

**Schedule**: Daily, 1-hour slots

**Scope**: Quick consultations, questions, guidance
