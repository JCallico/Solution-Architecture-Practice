# Career Development

## Overview

The Solution Architecture Practice is committed to developing architects into skilled, fulfilled professionals who drive business impact. We provide comprehensive support for career growth, learning, and advancement.

## Career Paths

### Architecture Track

```
Architect
    ↓
Senior Architect (3-5 years experience)
    ↓
Principal Architect (7-10 years experience)
    ↓
Distinguished Architect (10+ years experience)
```

**Architect**
- Independently design solutions
- Lead architecture reviews
- Mentor junior team members
- Contribute to practice knowledge

**Senior Architect**
- Mentor multiple architects
- Lead domain initiatives
- Influence practice standards
- Build partnerships with leadership
- Drive architectural decisions

**Principal Architect**
- Set architecture direction
- Lead major initiatives
- Shape practice strategy
- External representation
- Org-wide influence

**Distinguished Architect**
- Thought leadership
- Strategic advisor
- Practice innovation
- Industry contribution

### Technical Leadership Track

```
Individual Contributor
    ↓
Technical Lead (design complex systems)
    ↓
Principal Engineer (technical strategy)
    ↓
Distinguished Engineer (org-wide technical vision)
```

**Technical Lead**
- Lead technical design
- Own critical systems
- Set technical standards
- Mentor technical teams

**Principal Engineer**
- Technical strategy
- Cross-org influence
- Technology selection
- Innovation driver

**Distinguished Engineer**
- Org-wide technical vision
- Industry thought leadership
- Major innovation driver

### Practice Leadership Track

```
Architect
    ↓
Senior Architect with leadership interest
    ↓
Practice Manager / Director
    ↓
VP Architecture / Chief Architect
```

**Practice Manager**
- Team management
- Budget oversight
- Strategic planning
- Stakeholder relationships

**Director / VP**
- Practice strategy
- Org alignment
- Talent development
- Executive engagement

## Advancement Criteria

### Promotion Requirements

**Architect → Senior Architect**
- 3-5 years of architecture experience
- Demonstrated mastery of domain
- Mentored at least 2 junior architects
- Led 3+ major architectural initiatives
- Strong communication skills
- Peer recognition
- Leadership sponsorship

**Senior Architect → Principal Architect**
- 7-10 years of architecture experience
- Recognized expert in domain
- Mentored 5+ architects
- Led transformational initiatives
- Thought leadership contributions
- Industry engagement
- Executive visibility

**Principal → Distinguished**
- 10+ years of experience
- Recognized expert nationally/internationally
- Significant innovation contributions
- Major impact on business
- External thought leadership
- Organizational influence

### Evaluation Process

**Annual Review Cycle**

```
Q1: Goal Setting
├─ Career conversation with manager
├─ Define goals aligned with career path
├─ Identify skill gaps
└─ Plan development activities

Q2-Q3: Development
├─ Execute learning plan
├─ Seek feedback regularly
├─ Document achievements
└─ Adjust plan as needed

Q4: Evaluation
├─ Self-assessment
├─ Manager feedback
├─ Peer feedback
├─ Leadership assessment
└─ Plan next year
```

**360 Feedback**
- Peer feedback (colleagues)
- Manager feedback
- Team feedback (if applicable)
- Customer/stakeholder feedback
- Self-assessment
