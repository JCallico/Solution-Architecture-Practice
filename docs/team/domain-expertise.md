## Expertise Levels Defined

### Level 1: Familiar (< 1 year)
- Learning the domain
- Can describe key concepts
- Shadowing on small projects
- Questions and knowledge gaps
- **Example:** Architect new to company learning Azure

### Level 2: Competent (1-3 years)
- Independent work on projects
- Understands key patterns and anti-patterns
- Can mentor on specific topics
- Some gaps in advanced scenarios
- **Example:** Senior Architect comfortable with microservices but new to service mesh

### Level 3: Expert (3-8 years)
- Primary authority in domain
- Leads major design decisions
- Mentors multiple people
- Identifies and solves novel problems
- Publishes thought leadership
- **Example:** 5-year architect specializing in Kubernetes

### Level 4: Master (8+ years)
- Industry-recognized expert
- Sets standards and practices
- Keynote speaker capability
- Solves previously unsolved problems
- Long-term strategic vision
- **Example:** Principal Architect with 15 years AWS experience
