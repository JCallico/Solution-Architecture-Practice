# New Architect Onboarding

## Welcome to the Solution Architecture Practice! 

This 90-day onboarding plan will help you integrate into the team, learn our practices, and start contributing effectively as a solution architect.

## Onboarding Overview

### Your First 90 Days

- **Days 1-30**: Foundation & Learning
- **Days 31-60**: Practice & Contribution
- **Days 61-90**: Integration & Leadership

### Onboarding Buddy

You'll be paired with an experienced architect who will:
- Answer questions
- Provide guidance
- Review your work
- Help you navigate the organization

### Onboarding Checklist

Track your progress using the [Onboarding Checklist](./onboarding-checklist.md).

## Tips for Success

### Do's ✅
- **Ask Questions**: No question is too basic
- **Be Curious**: Dive deep into how things work
- **Seek Feedback**: Regularly ask for feedback
- **Build Relationships**: Get to know the team
- **Contribute Early**: Share your perspective
- **Take Notes**: Document your learnings
- **Be Patient**: Learning takes time

### Don'ts ❌
- **Don't Suffer in Silence**: Speak up if struggling
- **Don't Skip Reading**: Foundation is important
- **Don't Go Alone**: Leverage your buddy and team
- **Don't Rush**: Quality over speed
- **Don't Be Afraid to Say "I Don't Know"**: It's expected
