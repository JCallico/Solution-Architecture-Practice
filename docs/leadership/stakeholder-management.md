## ü§ù Relationship Building

### Building Trust
- Deliver consistently on commitments
- Be transparent about constraints and trade-offs
- Listen actively and empathetically
- Admit when you don't know
- Follow through on action items

### Regular Engagement
- **Executive 1:1s** - Monthly or bi-weekly
- **Team syncs** - Weekly or bi-weekly with key partners
- **Quarterly reviews** - Formal progress and planning sessions
- **Ad-hoc consultations** - Available for urgent needs
- **Social connections** - Coffee chats, team events

### Communication Strategies
- **Tailor message to audience** - Technical vs. business language
- **Lead with value** - Start with business impact
- **Be concise** - Respect their time
- **Use visuals** - Diagrams and dashboards over text
- **Follow up in writing** - Document decisions and actions

**Strategies:**
- **High Interest, High Influence** - Manage closely, key stakeholders
- **High Interest, Low Influence** - Keep informed, build advocates
- **Low Interest, High Influence** - Keep satisfied, demonstrate value
- **Low Interest, Low Influence** - Monitor, minimal effort

## üìà Managing Expectations

### Setting Clear Expectations
- Define scope and deliverables upfront
- Communicate timelines and dependencies
- Be realistic about capacity and capabilities
- Surface risks and constraints early
- Agree on success criteria

### Handling Conflicts
- Address disagreements directly and quickly
- Focus on shared goals and interests
- Find win-win solutions where possible
- Escalate appropriately when needed
- Document resolutions

## üíº Executive Presence

### Presenting to Executives
- **Start with the ask** - What decision do you need?
- **Tell the story** - Context, problem, solution, impact
- **Quantify value** - ROI, cost savings, revenue impact
- **Address risks** - Be transparent about challenges
- **Keep it brief** - 5-10 slides maximum

### Building Executive Relationships
- Understand their priorities and pressures
- Align architecture initiatives to business goals
- Provide regular, brief updates
- Bring solutions, not just problems
- Build credibility through delivery
