## ðŸŒŸ Leadership Philosophy

Our approach to practice leadership is built on:

1. **Servant Leadership** - Leaders serve the team and remove obstacles
2. **Technical Excellence** - Leading by example and maintaining technical credibility
3. **Strategic Influence** - Shaping organizational direction through architecture
4. **People Development** - Investing in team growth and career advancement
5. **Continuous Improvement** - Evolving practices based on feedback and learning

## ðŸ“Š Key Responsibilities

### Practice Lead
- Define practice vision, mission, and strategy
- Build and develop high-performing architecture team
- Establish governance frameworks and standards
- Drive architectural excellence across the organization
- Manage practice budget and resource allocation
- Build executive relationships and influence technology strategy

### Team Leads/Managers
- Manage day-to-day team operations
- Conduct performance reviews and career development
- Assign architects to projects and balance workload
- Foster team culture and collaboration
- Mentor junior architects
- Ensure quality of architectural deliverables

### Principal/Lead Architects
- Provide technical leadership on complex initiatives
- Define and evolve architecture standards
- Mentor other architects
- Lead Architecture Review Board
- Drive innovation and emerging technology adoption
- Represent architecture in cross-functional forums
