## ðŸ“‹ Key Principles

- **Loosely Coupled**: Services independent and can evolve separately
- **Highly Cohesive**: Related functionality grouped together
- **Resilient**: Design for failure, recovery, and scalability
- **Observable**: Monitor, log, trace, and alert on system behavior
- **Secure**: Defense in depth with multiple layers of protection
- **Cost-Conscious**: Optimize for operational and infrastructure costs
- **Automated**: Infrastructure as Code, CI/CD, and self-service
